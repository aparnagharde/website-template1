Catering

Food is the heart of any special occasion, and we understand that perfection is key. We pay close attention to your ideas and transform them into a delightful reality. Our talented chefs are skilled at crafting mouthwatering dishes. At our place, you can select from an extensive menu of over 300 beloved options or collaborate with us to design a dish that suits your taste. We cater to events ranging from intimate gatherings of 10 to grand celebrations of 10,000 or more, including outstation events, which is one of our specialties. After narrowing down your menu, we recommend scheduling a Menu Tasting Session. This not only ensures the food exceeds your expectations but also allows us to better understand your unique style!

Whatever event or service you choose, we will make it memorable. Let us focus on the food, while you focus on making great memories. Please call us to get in touch with one of our catering specialists to discuss further. We are providing following services:

  • Pickup
  • Delivery
  • Catering setup
  • On-site and live catering services
  • Including Family Style, Buffet, Plated, Butler Passed and Party Trays.
